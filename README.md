Tips for Implementation

    Understand the Theory: Before coding, ensure you understand the theoretical concepts behind each data structure and algorithm.
    Use Pseudocode: Write down pseudocode to outline your approach.
    Test Thoroughly: Create test cases to verify your implementation.
    Optimize: Analyze the time and space complexity of your implementations and look for optimization opportunities.
